Job Title

Description

We are looking for a designer to create engaging and on-brand graphics for a variety of media. We work with brands across multiple industries providing collaborative solutions to support their activities and campaigns by leveraging the Gojek ecosystem. You will also be an integral part of the brand building team focusing on marketing and promotional material to support the teams focus and activities.

What You'll Do

Planning concepts by studying relevant information and materials Illustrating concepts by designing examples of art/mock up arrangements, size, type and style and then submitting for approval Produce drafts for review by clients and make revisions based on the feedback received. Communicating and working closely with campaign designers and industry managers to deliver the right content for brand campaigns Coordinating with outside agencies, art services, marketing, printers as necessary. Producing consistently excellence visual work Working on internal and external newsletters and marketing content / promotions Ensuring your graphics capture the attention of those who see them and communicate the right message To assist in standardizing material across all industry verticals within brand partnerships portfolio

What You'll Need

3 years proven experience as a graphic designer or in related field Bachelor’s degree in graphic design, arts or related field Demonstrable graphic design skills with a strong portfolio Proficiency with required desktop publishing tools, including Photoshop, InDesign, Sketch and Illustrator (computer-aided design) Effective time management skills and the ability to meet tight deadlines Strong eye for visual composition and detail-oriented Able to give and receive constructive feedback and criticism Strong written and verbal communication skills The ability to think creatively and deliver impressive concepts
