Security & Privacy

Customer security and privacy are the main things to be handled properly. GreenHCM continues to maintain the security of all important customer data and information. Guaranteed servers, privacy data protection, periodic back-ups, and access to customer data, are GreenHCM's efforts to continue to maintain the security and privacy of every customer. GreenHCM ensures that all your information and data is encrypted and protected very well.

Central Data Security

This GreenHCM physical infrastructure uses a data center managed by Google and uses Google Cloud Platform (GCP) technology. Google data centers are accredited while continuously managing risks to ensure compliance with industry standards.

Google is highly experienced in building and managing large-scale data centers. This experience has been applied to the GCP platform and infrastructure. Physical and remote access is strictly guarded to the highest security standards.

For additional information, please visit: https://cloud.google.com/security

Personal

GreenHCM has a clear privacy policy that defines what data is collected and how it is used. GreenHCM is committed to safeguarding customer privacy and providing transparency on how GreenHCM works.

We take steps to protect the privacy of our customers by protecting the data stored on the platform. GreenHCM uses inherent system protection through authentication, access control, data transport encryption, and the use of HTTPS / SSL.

Backup

Systems and databases at GreenHCM are routinely backed up and stored in multiple locations and employ firewall technology to ensure that our services and your information are always safe and available, no matter what.

Customer Data Access

GreenHCM applies encryption technology to maintain the security of data and information stored so as to guarantee that your data will not be hacked, stolen, or used by irresponsible parties who are not related to GreenHCM.
